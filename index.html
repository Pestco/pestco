Spiders are arachnids that can become problematic pests when they infest homes or businesses. While most spiders are harmless to humans, some species can deliver painful bites or cause fear and discomfort, especially to those with arachnophobia. Identifying spiders typically involves recognizing their eight legs and two body segments, although their size, color, and markings can vary widely between species.

At Pestco, we specialize in providing effective spider pest control treatments to eliminate infestations and prevent their recurrence. Our services are designed to be safe, efficient, and tailored to the specific needs of our clients.

  • Interior and Exterior Treatment: We will apply targeted treatments to areas where spiders are likely to hide or breed, such as basements, attics, crawl spaces, and wall voids. This may involve the use of residual insecticides, dusts, or baits to effectively eliminate spiders and their prey.
  • Web Removal: We will remove existing spider webs and egg sacs from around your property to deter spiders from returning and to improve the overall appearance of your home or business.
  • Sealing Entry Points: We will identify and seal potential entry points, such as cracks, gaps, and openings in doors, windows, and walls, to prevent spiders from gaining access to your property.
  • Environmental Modifications: We may recommend environmental modifications, such as reducing clutter, eliminating sources of moisture, and minimizing outdoor lighting, to make your property less attractive to spiders and other pests.
  • Follow-Up Inspections: We offer follow-up inspections and treatments as needed to ensure that your spider problem is effectively resolved and to address any new infestations that may arise.

At Pestco, we understand the importance of providing safe and eco-friendly pest control solutions. That's why we use products and methods that are approved by regulatory authorities and are safe for use around children, pets, and the environment.

Rodents are mammals characterized by their continuously growing incisors that they must gnaw on to keep trimmed. They can become significant pests when they infest homes or businesses, causing damage to property, contaminating food, and transmitting diseases.

  • Inspection and Assessment: Our trained technicians will conduct a thorough inspection of your property to identify rodent entry points, nesting areas, and food sources.
  • Exclusion and Sealing: We will seal entry points such as gaps in walls, roofs, and foundations to prevent rodents from entering your property.
  • Trapping and Removal: We will strategically place traps to capture and remove rodents from your property safely and humanely.

  • Sanitation and Clean-up: We will advise you on proper sanitation practices to eliminate rodent attractants and remove rodent droppings and nesting materials.
