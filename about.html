About Us

For Reliable Pest Management.

Established in 2015, PESTCO has been serving the residents and businesses of Kerala with excellence in pest control solutions for over 9 years. With a passion for protecting homes, workplaces, and the environment, we have earned a reputation for reliability, professionalism, and effectiveness.

In the enchanted realm of pest control, where troubles crawl and flutter, PESTCO stands as the formidable sorcerer of solutions. Established in 2015, our journey spans over nine years in Kerala's embrace, with a passion for protecting homes, workplaces, and the environment. With a lineage steeped in ancient wisdom and modern expertise, our licensed enchanters wield their skills to unravel the most perplexing pest enigmas. Our reputation for reliability, professionalism, and effectiveness echoes through the years, a testament to our dedication. As we continue to serve with excellence, let our commitment be a beacon of hope in the ongoing battle against pests, safeguarding, protecting, and renewing for generations to come.

What We Do

At PESTCO, we specialize in providing comprehensive pest control services tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients. From residential properties to commercial establishments, we offer solutions for a wide range of pests, including rodents, insects, termites, and more. Our expert team employs advanced techniques and eco-friendly practices to ensure the safety and well-being of our customers while effectively eliminating pests.

Why Choose Us

  • Experience: With nearly a decade of experience in the industry, we have the knowledge and expertise to tackle any pest challenge.
  • Professionalism: Our team of licensed technicians is committed to delivering high-quality service with integrity and professionalism.
  • Customized Solutions: We understand that every pest problem is unique, which is why we tailor our solutions to address specific needs and concerns.
  • Safety First: The health and safety of our customers, their families, and the environment are our top priorities. We use safe and environmentally friendly pesticides and follow strict safety protocols in every job we undertake.
  • Customer Satisfaction: At PESTCO, customer satisfaction is paramount. We strive to exceed our customers' expectations by delivering prompt, reliable, and effective pest control solutions.

How We Work

  • Assessment: We begin by conducting a thorough inspection of your property to identify the type and extent of the pest infestation.
  • Treatment: Based on our assessment, we develop a customized treatment plan using the most appropriate methods and products to eliminate the pests.
  • Prevention: In addition to eradicating existing pests, we implement preventive measures to minimize the risk of future infestations, such as sealing entry points and providing ongoing maintenance services.
  • Education: We believe in empowering our customers with knowledge about pest control and prevention. Our technicians are happy to offer tips and advice on how to keep pests at bay between service visits.
